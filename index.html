Video games are a popular form of entertainment that combine storytelling, art, and technology to create interactive experiences. They range from simple puzzles to complex, immersive worlds where players can explore, compete, or collaborate with others. Over the years, video games have evolved significantly, becoming a major part of global culture and a thriving industry. Beyond just fun, they can also improve cognitive skills, encourage creativity, and foster social connections. Whether played casually or competitively, video games continue to shape the way people play, learn, and connect across the world.
